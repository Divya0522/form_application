Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a new form?

Click on “Get Started” and use the drag-and-drop builder to create a new form.

How to add different types of fields?

Select the field type (text, dropdown, checkbox, etc.) from the toolbar and drag it to your form.

Can I share the form with others?

Yes, you can copy the form link or embed it into your website.

What types of fields can I add?

You can add text fields, dropdowns, checkboxes, radio buttons, file uploads, date pickers, and more.

How do I make a field required?

Select the field, then toggle the “Required” option from the settings panel.

Can I add conditional logic to fields?

Yes, you can show or hide fields based on user responses using our logic rules panel.
